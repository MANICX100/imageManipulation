In digital imagery and indeed digital audio there are many types of compression schemes that can be grouped into either lossless or lossy compression.

Lossless Compression:

  • Information is re-organised
  • More information and therefore storage space required

Examples: PNG,GIF

Lossy Compression:

  • Information is discarded to reduce size
  • Image data can tolerate a certain amount of data loss
  • Only an approximation of the image can be reconstructed
  • Less information and therefore storage space required

Examples: JPEG
